Council is a game of knowing, choosing, and consequence — played with words,
not dice. Every answer comes one of two ways: **seized at a price**, or
**given as grace**. Behind every locked door wait three clues, so no soul is
ever truly stuck. It is built to reward **hope**.

The full handbook — its several books — is being bound. Chapters will be
added below as they are finished.
