Council was not written all at once. It began as an argument about what a table
loses when it sets down the dice, and became — draft by draft, ruling by ruling —
a whole system. This is that record: the work kept in the order it was made, so
you can watch the game get built.

Read any document in your browser, or take the printable copy. The finished,
revised rules live at **The Handbook** as they are settled; what follows is the
workshop behind it.
